DELIVERY POLICY

Different shipping methods are available based on the products purchased and the delivery location. Each shipping method has its own restrictions and charges that will be applied to your order. Business days are Monday-Friday, excluding federal holidays within the United States.
48 Contiguous United States
1.Standard Shipping - Most orders arrive within 2-3 days (Pharmacy items may take longer) and may be shipped to the 48 contiguous United States. Please note: special orders, military addresses and P.O. Boxes may exceed the standard shipping days.
2.Guaranteed 2-Day Shipping Most products may be shipped to the 48 contiguous United States via second-day service and arrive within 2 days. Orders must be placed before 12:00 p.m. (noon) ET or your order may not be processed until the following day.
3.Guaranteed Next-Day Shipping Most products may be shipped to the 48 contiguous United States via overnight service and arrive within 1 day. Orders must be placed before 12:00 p.m. (noon) ET or your order may not be processed until the following day.
Alaska or Hawaii
1.Second Day (2-3 business days*) - Most products may be shipped to Alaska or Hawaii via Second Day Service and arrive within 2-3 business days.
2.U.S. Postal Service (3-8 business days*) Most products may be shipped via U.S. Postal Service, but some restrictions apply. Delivery is expected to arrive within 3-8 business days. The following products cannot be shipped via U.S Postal Service at this time: products over 70 lbs., products with large dimensions, freight-only products and flammable products.

RETURNS POLICY

Returning Online Purchases
If you're not satisfied with you’re The Durham House of Pets purchase, you can return the product for the purchase price (minus the shipping, handling, gift wrap and other charges). Product must be in its original packaging.
In Store
To return an item in store that was purchased at The Durham House of Pets, simply bring the product in its original packaging plus the packing slip or the shipping confirmation email as proof of purchase to any PetSmart store.
By Mail
Pack the item safely and securely for shipping and include the packing slip (retain a copy for your records). Affix the RETURN shipping label provided and send the package via a traceable and insurable method. Please note: exchanges are not available by mail. All shipping charges must be paid by the customer
Returning Products Purchased at a The Durham House of Pets If you're not satisfied with a product purchased in the store, simply bring the product in its original packaging and in new or salable condition to any The Durham House of Pets to be returned.
